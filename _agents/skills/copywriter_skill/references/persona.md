# User Persona: The Recruitment Architect

## The Emotional State: High-Stakes Pressure
- **Context:** Managing a National/State-level nursing recruitment (AIIMS, ESIC, etc.).
- **Pressure:** 10,000+ candidates viewing the configuration. Any logic error leads to legal challenges (HC/SC petitions).
- **Time:** Highly hurried. They want "Zero-Click" clarity.

## The Mental Map: What they are Thinking
- "Did I set the cut-off date right? AIIMS uses Jan 1st, ESIC uses the last date of application."
- "How do I explain these relaxations so I don't have to answer 100 emails?"
- "Is the Ex-Servicemen logic legally compliant?"

## Semantic Alignment: Word Choice
- **Prefer:** *Fairness, Alignment, Recognition, Anchor, Equitable, Recognition.*
- **Avoid:** *Barrier, Restriction, Limit, Data, Constraint, Vector.*

## The Role of the AI Copywriter
The AI should act as a **Reliable Advisor**. It shouldn't just "label" fields; it should "Assure" the Admin that the logic is sound and the language is human-ready.
