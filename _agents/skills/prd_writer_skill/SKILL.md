# PRD Writer Skill: The Master Intelligence Orchestrator

## Purpose: The Absolute Zero-Ambiguity Mandate
This skill is the "Lead Architect" of the configuration engine. It ensures that every Product Requirement Document (PRD) is a "Perfect Science"—an absolute, zero-failure guide that leaves no click, state, or logic-gate to chance. It formally integrates **Nine Power-Specialist Layers** to create a bulletproof blueprint for any designer or developer.

---

## THE MASTER "BIBLE" STRUCTURE (Standard Template)
To eliminate 100% of ambiguity, every PRD generated by this skill MUST follow this exhaustive deep-dive structure:

### 1. Executive Vision & Objectives
- **Strategic Objective**: What is the singular goal?
- **Root Cause & Problem Check**: Exactly what chronic pain point are we solving? (Trigger: `Deep Clarity`)

### 2. Audience & Persona Layer (The Human Truth)
- **For Whom**: Detailed discovery of the "Fan Persona" (clinical context, stress levels, daily life).
- **User Needs**: What does success look like for the human user? (Trigger: `User Journey Architect`)

### 3. Functional Blueprint (The Logic Engine)
- **Functional Requirements**: Detailed list of what the system *must* do.
- **Component Registry**: For EVERY UI component (Input, Button, Toggle):
  - **Definition**: What is it?
  - **States**: `Idle | Focused | Active | Success | Error`.
  - **Input Logic**: Regex blocks, non-negative constraints, mandatory vs. optional. (Trigger: `Logic Builder Architect`)
- **Functional Flow**: The "Happy Path" vs "Rescue Flows."

### 4. Experience Blueprint (The Sensory Design)
- **Visual Language**: Colors (Hex), Typography, Design Constraints.
- **Micro-Interactions & Choreography**: Exact `framer-motion` specs for every state transition. (Trigger: `UX Choreographer`)
- **Verbal Identity**: Zomato-style headlines, humanized feedback, and the **System Response Dictionary**. (Trigger: `Elite Copywriter` + `Tone Harmonizer`)

### 5. Reliability Blueprint (The Perfection Shield)
- **Edge Case Exhaustion**: Audit of every "What-if" scenario. (Trigger: `Edge Case Auditor`)
- **Acceptance Criteria (AC)**: The definitive checklist of "Success" per component. (Trigger: `AC Mapper`)
- **Pre-Flight Simulation**: Results of the dummy notification stress-test. (Trigger: `Logic Simulator`)
