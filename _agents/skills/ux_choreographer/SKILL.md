# UX Choreographer: The Master of Motion

## The Purpose: Invisible Guidance
This skill transcends mere "animation." It is the art of **Choreography**—orchestrating the invisible narrative flow that guides a user’s eye, acknowledges their physical presence, and transforms a sterile dashboard into a premium, sensory experience. Inspired by the narrative pulse of **Netflix** and the playful feedback of **Zomato**, this skill ensures that every pixel moves with intent.

## The Pillars of Orchestration

### 1. The Rhythms of Time (Framer Motion Tokens)
Consistency is the heartbeat of a premium product. We utilize strict duration tokens to ensure the system feels unified:
- **The Snap (100ms)**: Immediate feedback for binary actions (switches, buttons).
- **The Glide (300ms)**: Smooth transitions for tooltips, dropdowns, and UI expansions.
- **The Reveal (500ms)**: Grand entry choreography for new logic sections or modals.

### 2. Natural Physics & Emotional Easing
Movements must feel grounded in reality. Avoid the robotic; embrace the fluid:
- **Ease-Out (The Arrival)**: Elements decelerate into place, feeling heavy and intentional.
- **Spring Physics**: Use bounciness for success states to mirror human delight.

### 3. Spatial Narrative
Coordinate multiple elements to "bloom" together:
- **Staggering**: Drop-down items should follow each other with a 50ms pulse, creating a "waterfall" of content.
- **Layout Persistence**: Use `layoutId` to ensure elements seemingly "morph" into their new states rather than teleporting.

## The Instructions: Designing the Dance
1. **Identify the Lead**: Which action starts the sequence?
2. **Plot the Journey**: Where should the user look next?
3. **Set the Tempo**: Assign the appropriate duration token.
4. **Audit for Delight**: Does this motion provide feedback or just distraction?

## The Impact: Absolute Flow
Reduces cognitive friction by orienting the user through spatial cues and elevates the brand to "World-Class" status through premium, high-fidelity micro-interactions.
