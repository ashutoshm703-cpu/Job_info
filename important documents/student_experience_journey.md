# Student Experience Journey: The Candidate Pipeline

This document outlines the end-to-end flow of a candidate evaluating their eligibility for a specific nursing recruitment.

---

### 🧠 Logic & Discussion Points

#### **1. The "Flag" vs. "Fail" Logic**
- **FAIL**: Hard rejection (e.g., age is 50 when cap is 40).
- **FLAG**: A "soft" warning or caution (e.g., you are from out-of-state and need to transfer registration, or you are a Govt employee and need to check your group).

#### **2. High-Density Results**
Instead of a simple "Yes/No," the student sees the *reasoning* behind the age calculation (e.g., mentioning the female matrix or the PwBD boost).

#### **3. Action Routing**
The student is immediately routed either back to their inputs (to correct a mistake) or forward to the official application portal with the PDF source ready for reference.
